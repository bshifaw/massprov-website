A warm, welcoming improv community

MassProv supports improvisers across Massachusetts.

We’re a community of improvisers connecting people through events, workshops, jams, and a supportive WhatsApp network. Find indie shows, jams, workshops, and resources to help you grow and perform your best.

What we do

Promote indie improv shows and jams, share stage/practice space options, and connect performers with education opportunities.

Who it’s for

Improvisers in Massachusetts and nearby areas, from brand new players to experienced teams, looking for shows, rehearsals, or community.

How to engage

Submit events, browse resources, attend shows and jams, and join the WhatsApp community for fast updates.
