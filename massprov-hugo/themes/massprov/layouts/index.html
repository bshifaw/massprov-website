A warm, welcoming improv community

MassProv supports improvisers across Massachusetts.

MassProv is a community group supporting improvisers across Massachusetts. We share a monthly newsletter and host an active WhatsApp network to help people find indie shows, jams, workshops, and collaborators. Our goal is to make it easier for improvisers to stay connected, perform, and grow together.

Contact / Join WhatsApp View Calendar

What we do

We highlight indie improv shows and jams, share practice and stage opportunities, and keep a running pulse on what’s happening in the community. We also occasionally partner with instructors or teams to help promote workshops and special events.

Who it’s for

Improvisers in Greater Boston and nearby areas, from brand-new players to seasoned performers, looking for shows, rehearsals, collaborators, or community.

How to engage

Join the WhatsApp community, subscribe to the newsletter, submit your events, and come out to shows and jams. Share what you’re working on, support other performers, and use MassProv as a hub to stay connected.

How to engage

Warm-ups, workshops, and active collaborators.

Share rehearsal spaces, swap new exercises, and find other improvisers looking to coach, guest, or jam together. MassProv is a space to try things out and keep your practice steady.
